Model United Nations

Members of the Young Visionary Club proudly represented our school at the Model United Nations, hosted by the Rotary Club of Central Port of Spain, on November 8th, 2025. They showcased exceptional leadership, critical thinking, and diplomacy as they participated in global discussions and debates. The club commends them for their dedication, vision, and excellence in representing both the school and the Young Visionary spirit.
